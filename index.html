How It Works

  1. Click on "Submit Your Data" to fill in your CBAT examination details
  2. If you've already submitted, click "Get Your Report" to view your scores
  3. Your T-Score is calculated based on candidates with the same Zone, Date, and Shift
  4. The Overall Score is calculated as: (Sum of T-Scores × 30) / 400
